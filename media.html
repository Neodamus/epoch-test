Date: August, 20, 2013

This is the first update for EoE! Today we will be revealing five Elements and three Units to give you a solid taste of the diversity and uniqueness of units and also reveal some of the gameplay and mechanics of Epoch of Elements!

Be sure to check out the Illusionist update! (My favorite unit :))

Elements:

Fire Wind Earth Lightning Water

The aggressive Charger.

In EoE, all units have a limited amount of attacks, defends, and movement

points per turn. The Charger is a fairly 'tanky' unit with a unique ability called, "Multiple Attacks". This ability is passively applied to the Charger, and it allows him to attack an additional two times each turn, this makes the Charger an exceptionally powerful unit, especially when combined with buffs or other support abilities.

The sturdy Vangaurd.

The Vangaurd is an extremely well rounded unit, although, like the Charger,

he has a 'tanky' feel, but he has another important role as a counter to

stealthed units and traps. He can reveal units and traps within a two hexgrid

area around himself, and if he spots an enemy he has a passive ability

called, "Engulf", that applies a debuff on attack, which deals one pure-damage

per turn and prevents the attacked unit from stealthing for the duration of

the debuff.

The tricky Illusionist.

Last but not least, the Illusionist. He is a fairly weak unit in terms of dealing

and receiving damage, but he makes up for it in deceptiveness. The Illusionist has

two abilities, the first one is called, "Mirror Image", it creates a new unit as an illusion

of the targeted ally unit. Illusions retain all stats, so at first sight, your opponent will

not know if it is real or fake. Illusions can only deal a max of five damage, which is

enough to harass units, but not deal any real damage, and Illusions also receive

(2x) damage from any source, making them a very fragile unit. The second ability is

called, "Smokescreen", and it can be extremely useful if used correctly, but it can

also be extremely dangerous if your opponent takes advantage by hiding inside or

behind it. Smokescreen has an area of affect range of one, and units standing

directly in the center cannot normally be seen by other units; however, units standing

inside smoke screen have their Sight, Range, and Reveal stats to zero,

units can only cast abilities or move while inside smokescreen.
