What is Epoch of Elements?

EoE is a modern turn based strategy game which is played online in a one versus one setting. Inspiration for this game came from Heroes of Might&Magic, Stratego(Board game), and Dota2.

   

What makes it fun?

The number and diversity of Hero-like-units, units, abilities, buffs, and auras allows each player to have personalized strategies and unique tactics. It's a battle of small decisions, huge decisions, strategies, tactics, mind games, innovation, combos, and counter-picking.

   

What are the future plans for EoE?

We are currently planning on where EoE is headed, there are a few important decisions that have to be made by us and the community. We could go with a browser based HTML5 path or we can go as a stand alone game which could be found on indie game promotion websites such as Steam's Greenlight-Indie Games. Both paths have their own challenges, but for now we're trying our best to put out a playable game for everyone!
